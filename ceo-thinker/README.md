CEO Thinker elevates your thinking to the level of elite CEOs like Satya Nadella, Jeff Bezos, and Elon Musk. It helps you:

- **Think in first principles** - Break problems to fundamentals and rebuild from scratch
- **See systems, not silos** - Map interactions, dependencies, and second-order effects
- **Demand evidence** - Base decisions on data, not opinions or assumptions
- **Spot cognitive biases** - Detect and counteract mental shortcuts that distort reasoning
- **Ask contrarian questions** - Explore non-obvious angles and challenge conventional wisdom
- **Optimize long-term** - Think in 3-5 year arcs that compound over time
- **Enforce clarity** - Simplify complexity until the core insight is obvious
- **Iterate with high standards** - Combine perfectionism with rapid testing

## How It Works

### 1. Start with First Principles
Every analysis begins by stripping away assumptions and identifying fundamental truths.

### 2. Demand Evidence
No claims without data. Every assertion requires supporting evidence.

### 3. Think in Systems
Map interactions, feedback loops, and cascading effects.

### 4. Check for Biases
Actively hunt for cognitive distortions: confirmation bias, sunk cost fallacy, groupthink, etc.

### 5. Ask Contrarian Questions
"What if the opposite is true?" - Explore dismissed ideas and non-obvious angles.

### 6. Optimize Long-Term
Evaluate every decision on 3-5 year horizons, not quarterly cycles.

### 7. Enforce Clarity
If you can't explain it in one sentence, you don't understand it yet.

### 8. Kill Bad Ideas
Challenge your own conclusions. Admit when you're wrong. Update beliefs when evidence changes.
